A Literary Home for Writers and World Literati

ARAWLII is dedicated to literature, scholarship, cultural memory and global intellectual dialogue. It brings together writers, poets, researchers, translators and readers through meaningful literary initiatives.

Who We Are

An Academy Shaped by Literature and Human Experience

ARAWLII stands for the Academy of Writers and World Literati. It is a platform created for the celebration, development and preservation of literary and intellectual work.

The academy recognises literature as more than written expression. Literature carries personal memories, cultural identities, social realities, historical experiences and the ideas of every generation.


Through publications, author profiles, research, literary discussions and creative projects, ARAWLII seeks to create a respectful space where writers and readers can connect with one another.

01

Literary Community

A welcoming platform for established authors, emerging writers, scholars, poets and literary readers.

02

Intellectual Exchange

A space for thoughtful conversations, critical inquiry, research and the exchange of literary ideas.

03

Cultural Memory

An effort to preserve stories, biographies, documents and literary contributions for future generations.

Why ARAWLII Matters

Literature Connects Memory, Identity and Imagination

Every writer contributes to a larger human story. Every literary work reflects a time, a place, a struggle, a question or a dream.

ARAWLII exists to recognise those contributions and to support the people behind them. It values both creative writing and serious academic inquiry.
